# Test Cases: Google Trends

- What were the top 5 rising search terms in the New York City last month?
- Compare the top ranked term in Japan vs Brazil for the most recent available month.
- List the terms with the highest percent gain in the UK on September 1 this year.
- When did the latest top three terms start trending and what is their month-over-month growth rate?
- What is the ratio of the top term's score to the average score of all terms last quarter?
- Which terms had a score higher than the average score of all terms on 2025-10-01?
- Find terms that were in the top 10 in both the US and UK on 2025-10-01.
- What is the rank difference for 'Taylor Swift' between the US and UK on 2025-10-01?
- What is the date range of all data in this dataset? Within that range, what month has the most trends data? Within that month, what was the biggest trend and how big was it in relation to the others?
